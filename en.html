Founder and CEO Raphael Schwarz very early in his life discovered his passion for computer science. He founded this first company, while still being employed, at the age of 18. In 2011 decided to focus on his own business entirely and founded codeworx. Raphael shows the ever-growing passion for his occupation as speaker at various international conferences on a regular basis.

codeworx

As a service company in the domain of custom software development, codeworx supports their partners and clients since 2011 with their expertise in consulting, coaching, project implementation and workshops.

Individuality is key: Every client is as unique as the projects we are planning an implementing with them. That is why not only knowing your client but being in a constant information exchange with them, for codeworx is simply a matter of course.

No matter if short-term consulting contracts or long-term project coachings: Our focus will be to bring the best service possible to our clients and their needs.

Services

The service portfolio of codeworx is grouped in four main categories.

Consulting

Our consulting services are one of our core competencies. We assist our clients and partners by providing precise a specialized support for various problems they might run into. Our claim is to no just find and point at problems but mainly provide solutions for them.

Project coaching

Compared to our mostly short-term consulting services, our project coaching is of a more long-term nature. It is designed to offer support to our client’s development teams, especially when they hit dead ends or experience complicated technical difficulties they need to overcome. Our highest priority in those situations is to avoid the proverbial production of just “hot air”. The codeworx approach is: If everyone else is telling you what you are doing wrong, we show you how to fix it.

Implementation

Another important pillar of our success is, that we also provide hands on implementation services for parts or the whole of our client’s projects. Especially when there is a shortage of internal resources it is reassuring to have a strong partner to lend a helping hand. Depending on the timeframe and the requested scope of our services we try to provide you the pest possible package to help you succeed.

Workshops

Our training programs are optimized to provide in-house trainings for your employees. All our trainings are individually composed to suit you specific need perfectly.

The workshops can be held as an on-site as well as a remote training via videoconference. We do our best to share our extensive technical knowledge combined with the necessary hands-on experience with the attendees and help them lower the entry threshold to new technologies.
